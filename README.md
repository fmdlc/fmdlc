Site Reliability Engineer with 15+ years of experience building and scaling robust, secure, and resilient infrastructure for mission-critical and high-load environments.

I design systems that deliver reliability, performance, and scalability at global scale, with a strong focus on automation, operational excellence, and platforms that reduce toil while enabling teams to move faster.

## About

I use this space to collect writing, talks, and technical work around reliability, large-scale systems, ELF binaries, dynamic linking, debugging, observability, and operational design.

**Focus**

- Site Reliability Engineering and platform engineering
- Resilient infrastructure for high-load and mission-critical systems
- Automation, observability, and self-healing operational models
- Linux, ELF, loaders, linkers, and runtime behavior
- Low-level debugging, security research, and binary analysis
